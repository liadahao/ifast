Tours4Tech offers a range of study tours in Shenzhen that allow you to experience real-life business not only in the tech industry, but also manufacturing and market trade. How To Work In Tech In Shenzhen

Whether you are a returning Chinese student from overseas or an expat who wants to get a taste of the tech excitement, this workshop will prepare you for entering the tech industry in Shenzhen. We cover how to go about getting a job, where to apply, how to prepare a resume, how to improve your chances of getting hired, and how to give an unforgettable interview that is specific to the tech industry.
